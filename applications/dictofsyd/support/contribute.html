Contribute

If you have information, research, personal stories, or images that you would like to contribute to the Dictionary of Sydney, please let us know.

To send us a message or suggestion, click on the Contact Us link on any page.

We may not be able to include your contribution, depending on length, other commissions or suitability, but we'd like to see it.

  • Write a short summary of your contribution in the text box
  • Check your contact details are correct so that we can get back to you
  • Before sending, type the two words you see in the reCAPTCHA box into the field provided – this protects the site from spammers.
