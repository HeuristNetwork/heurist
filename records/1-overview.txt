
Directory:	/records

Overview:	This directory contains all functions relating to the access and editing
		    of database records, including the capture of information from web pages through the bookmarklet.

Notes:		Record editing primarily acts on the records, bookmarks and details tables.

		    Also see /import for other methods of getting data into Heurist.
