Please help us help you!

We operate in 'continuous beta'. That means that we make small incremental improvements and bug-fixes on an almost daily basis.

That means you may occasionally encounter small glitches in the interface*

The other side of the coin is that we can, and do, fix bugs often in a matter of hours.

So if you encounter a problem - either something which doesn't work or an issue of workflow or some step which isn't clear or you simply don't understand how it works - please shoot us an email immediately.

Don't wait until the issue bugs you. You are not 'bothering' us in any way with small problems, you are helping us understand where other users might also have a problem so that we can tweak, or fix, the interface.

* our underlying database model and data engine are entirely stable
